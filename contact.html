A Few Questions

Before you reach out…

Is the discovery call really free?

Yes — 30 minutes, no cost, no pressure. It's the only way to know if we're a fit.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the scope. A DNA consultation can be done in a week. A full biological family search can take three to twelve months. I'll give you an honest timeline before you commit.

What if you find something hard?

I deliver hard findings with care, gentleness, and at your pace. You'll never be ambushed. We talk first about what you'd want to know — and how — before I share anything significant.

Do you work with clients outside Florida?

Absolutely. I work with families across the United States and internationally. Everything is done by phone, video, and email — wherever you are.
