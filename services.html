Services

Five thoughtful ways to dig.

Whether you're searching for a person, building a tree, or trying to make sense of a pattern — there's a path here for you.

Family Tree & Ancestry Research

A carefully sourced, document-backed family tree — built from census records, vital records, church archives, immigration manifests, and oral histories. Going as far back as the records allow, with every claim cited.

Starting at $850 · Custom scope available

Biological Family Search

Locating birth parents, biological siblings, or unknown relatives — for adoptees, donor-conceived individuals, and anyone with a missing branch. DNA analysis, paper trails, and discreet outreach.

Starting at $1,200 · Quoted per case

Legacy Story Reports

A beautifully written, illustrated narrative of your family — not just names and dates, but the lives behind them. A keepsake to pass on. Printed and bound, or delivered as a digital book.

Starting at $1,500 · Includes printed copy

DNA Guidance

Confused by your AncestryDNA, 23andMe, or MyHeritage results? I'll help you read the matches, understand ethnicity estimates, and turn surprising results into a clear next step.

$250 · 90-minute consultation

Identity & Generational Coaching

Once we know what's there, what does it mean? A guided conversation about generational patterns, inherited gifts, family stories — and how all of it shapes the life you're building now.

$185 / session · Packages available
A Note on Pricing

Every family is different. So is every quote.

The prices above are starting points. Real costs depend on the depth of research, the records available, and the size of the family branch we're chasing. After our discovery call, I'll send you a clear written proposal with a flat fee — no hourly surprises.

Not sure which service is right for you? That's exactly what the discovery call is for. We'll talk it through, and I'll point you to the path that actually fits your situation.
